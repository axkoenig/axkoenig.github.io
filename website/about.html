Alexander Koenig

Biography

I am pursuing my PhD in TU Berlin's Robotics and Biology Lab, where Prof. Oliver Brock advises me. Previously, I worked as a Research Fellow in the Harvard Biorobotics Lab and the Mechatronics in Medicine Lab at Imperial College London, and as a Machine Learning Researcher at Merantix in Berlin. I did my master's in Robotics, Cognition, Intelligence and my bachelor's in Engineering Science, both at TU Munich.

My current research focuses on new control and sensing paradigms for soft robotic hands to unlock robust object manipulation. I am involved in various audio-visual art projects. I'm an avid photographer, I'm one half of Aqua Veen, and I'm collaborating with Justin Urbach and others on BLINDHÆD, a multi-media art installation based on event vision.
