PROJEKT 07 ongoing

For my Master Thesis in the Harvard Biorobotics Lab I am investigating autonomous control algorithms for tactile grasp refinement. We compare analytic methods with approaches based on deep reinforcement learning. — with Prof Robert Howe and Prof Björn Menze

PROJEKT 06 Summer 2020

I worked on COVID-19 detection from chest radiographs as part of the Deep Learning in Medical Imaging course at the Department of Biomedical Engineering at Tel Aviv Universtiy. We use autoencoders trained on healthy lung images to detect anomalies in images from infected patients. — with Prof Greenspan

PROJEKT 05 Summer 2020

I took part in the Machine Learning in Computer Graphics practical offered by the Blavatnik School of Computer Science at Tel Aviv Universtiy. In a small team we developed a new method for self-supervised class and content disentanglement. — with Prof Cohen-Or

PROJEKT 04 Winter 2019

In my master's I took part in TUM's software-engineering practical iPraktikum. In cooperation with an industry partner our team built the community-based application LocalHero for the German Red Cross. The system connects people in need of general assistance or with urgent medical emergencies with helping users around them. — with Prof Brügge

PROJEKT 03 Winter 2019

In the master's seminar Applied Deep Learning in Natural Language Processing at the chair for Political Data Science I improved an existing approach to generate word embeddings. I used this system to compare gender bias in religious texts. — with Prof Hegelich

PROJEKT 02 Summer 2019

During my research internship at the Mechatronics in Medicine Lab at Imperial College London I built a modular ROS platform to intuitively control a robotic rig with a gesture tracking device. I integrated virtual reality support into the system and played with some ideas regarding autonomous robotic grasping. — with Prof Rodriguez y Baena

PROJEKT 01 Summer 2018

For my Bachelor Thesis at the NARVIS Lab I developed an application for the Microsoft HoloLens to support orthopedic trauma surgeons with intra-operative 3D visualisations of complex bone fractures. I conducted a user study with four trauma surgeons to evaluate my work. — with Prof Navab
