    Biografie

     I am a master's student in Robotics, Cognition, Intelligence at the Technical University of Munich (TUM). I am currently working on my master thesis at the Harvard Biorobotics Lab as a Fellow of the Harvard School of Engineering and Applied Sciences. I hold a B.Sc. in Engineering Science, an interdisciplinary engineering program from TUM. My main interests are machine learning applications, computer vision/graphics, robotics, and medical technology. Browse through some of my projects in the research tab and download a short version of my CV here.
    I love taking carefully engineered photographs. My photos often reflect my passion for architecture. All the photos were shot on a Leica M3 with a Summicron-M f2 from the 1950s. All images on this website are copyright © 2021 Alexander Koenig. If you would like to use the photos and get high-res versions, please get in touch. This website was last updated in September 2021.
